Quick start

Copy this snippet and paste it into your HTML where you want the map to appear:

<iframe
  src="https://map.opshopsearch.com/?embed=1"
  width="100%"
  height="500"
  frameborder="0"
  loading="lazy"
  title="Op shops on the Gold Coast"></iframe>

Filter examples

Show only your charity's shops:

<iframe src="https://map.opshopsearch.com/?embed=1&charity=vinnies"
        width="100%" height="500" frameborder="0" loading="lazy"></iframe>

Show only one suburb:

<iframe src="https://map.opshopsearch.com/?embed=1&suburb=burleigh-heads"
        width="100%" height="500" frameborder="0" loading="lazy"></iframe>

Open a specific shop's panel on load:

<iframe src="https://map.opshopsearch.com/?embed=1&shop=lifeline-shop-burleigh-heads"
        width="100%" height="500" frameborder="0" loading="lazy"></iframe>

URL parameters

ParameterValuesDescription
embed1Required. Strips header, menu, install prompt, and overflow buttons.
charitycharity slug (e.g. vinnies, salvos, lifeline)Pre-filters the map to one charity. Browse all charity slugs.
suburbsuburb slug (e.g. burleigh-heads)Pre-filters the map to one suburb. Browse all suburb slugs.
shopshop slug (e.g. vinnies-burleigh-heads)Auto-opens that shop's detail panel.

FAQ

Will I be charged? No. The embed is free. We have no plans to monetise it.

Can I customise the colours? Not yet. The embed inherits your visitor's system light/dark preference. Custom theming is on the roadmap — let us know what you need at hello@opshopsearch.com.

Will my visitors be tracked? No. We don't run analytics or third-party trackers. The only state stored is the visitor's own favourites and view preferences (in their browser's localStorage, scoped to map.opshopsearch.com).

Can I get a logo or co-branding? Right now the embed shows a small "Powered by OpShop Finder" badge in the bottom-right. Get in touch if you want to discuss a partnership listing.
